Product Care
How do I clean my Urban Ummati piece?
Use a soft, dry cloth to gently remove dust.
Avoid:
● Water
● Harsh chemicals
● Abrasive cleaners
● Wet cloths
● Sprays
● Solvents
● Excessive pressure
For delicate finishes, test any cleaning method on a small hidden area first.
Can the artwork be exposed to sunlight?
Avoid prolonged direct sunlight, as it may affect colours, finishes, wood, adhesives, or materials
over time.
Can I place the artwork in a bathroom or humid area?
Unless clearly stated, we do not recommend placing Urban Ummati products in bathrooms,
damp basements, near humidifiers, or areas with high moisture.
Humidity can affect wood, adhesives, finishes, and layered materials.

What should I do if my piece has LED lighting?
If your product includes LED lighting, follow the care and safety instructions provided with that
product.
Do not expose LED components, wiring, power supplies, or connectors to water or moisture.
Only use the recommended power supply and components.
